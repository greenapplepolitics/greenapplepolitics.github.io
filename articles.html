Apple v. FBI

By Jack Flanagan

Every few years there is a court case that captures the attention of a country. From OJ Simpson in 1995 to the Supreme Court ruling on gay marriage in 2015, some cases are powerful enough to define a nation's identity. We are seeing this again in 2016; Apple v FBI has engaged the country in a way never seen before. It takes a lot for a legal battle to populate the minds of most Americans: constitutional rights, security, relevancy, and a host of other topics.
